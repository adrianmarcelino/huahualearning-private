## 5 light-palette references

1. **fromanother.love** (SOTD 2026-05-14, Dev Award) — kinetic typography hero, scroll-staged
   reveals. Move applied: phone mockup sticky-pin paired with stepped narrative panels.

2. **Happly (gethapply.com)** (SOTD 2026-05-12, Dev Award) — warm-neutral palette + playful spring
   motion + custom illustration accents. Move applied: 3D mascot adopts spring poses on reactions
   instead of static decoration.

3. **Mads Matters (better-environment-654310.framer.app)** — minimal light palette + single accent
   color. Move applied: sage stays primary accent; gold reserved only for value/price/hook.

4. **XOX (xox.makemepulse.com)** (SOTD 2026-05-10, Dev Award) — light bg with bold WebGL accents.
   Move applied: ambient WebGL canvas at 0.55 opacity over cream, deeper than 30% kills readability.

5. **ASTRODITHER (astrodither.robertborghesi.is)** (SOTD 2026-05-05, Dev Award) — light palette
   with grain texture + retro grid. Move applied: 4% noise overlay on hero (already in globals.css
   `.bg-noise`).
